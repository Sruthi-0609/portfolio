About Me

The Foundation My journey into technology began with a fascination for the "why" behind the digital world. Currently a pre-final year Information Technology student, I have spent my academic career diving into the core logic of systems. While many find subjects like Information and Coding Theory to be purely mathematical, I see them as the blueprint of our digital future. This technical foundation allows me to approach development not just as a coder, but as a problem-solver who understands the architecture beneath the interface.

The Creative Spark While my degree provided the logic, my passion for Frontend Development provided the medium. I realized early on that even the most powerful backend system is only as good as its user interface. This sparked my transition into a creator of digital experiences. I began mastering HTML5 and CSS3 as tools for storytelling, focusing on how a website can be both functional and beautiful. To me, a great website is like a conversation: it should be clear, engaging, and leave the user feeling heard.
